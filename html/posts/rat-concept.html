Radio Access Technology (RAT)

A Radio Access Technology (RAT) is the underlying physical connection method for a radio based communication network.

Many modern mobile phones support several RATS in one device such as Bluetooth, Wi-Fi and GSM, UMTS, LTE or 5G NR.

The term RAT was traditionally used in mobile communication network interoperability.

The term is used when a user device selects between the type of RAT being used to connect to the Internet. This is often performed similar to access point selection in IEEE 802.11 (Wi-Fi) based networks.
